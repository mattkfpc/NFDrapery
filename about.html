Our Story

Quality workmanship and customer service have always been the cornerstone of our philosophy at NFDrapery. From our humble beginnings to the industry wide scale of our business today, our founders, Ilman and Malrae Kim distinguished themselves in the design industry by their industrious work ethic, attention to detail, and creative offerings.

We are a full product and service workroom dedicated to our clients by providing the best window treatment or design solutions. From the initial consultation to the finished product, NFDrapery works closely with designers to create innovative designs and beautiful products.

While our business has grown and expanded over the years, our culture and message remain the same: We will always strive for perfection and become an innovative workroom that our designers could be proud to call their own.
